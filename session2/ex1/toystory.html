Woody

Woody is the same pull-string cowboy sheriff that Andy fell in love with years
ago. He's found a new home with Bonnie and her toys, and he'll do anything
to make sure she's happy and they're all taken care of. But an unexpected
reunion with his dear friend Bo Peep shows Woody that the world is much
bigger than he ever imagined.
